How to Use PharmCalc

Basic Calculator

Enter grades for predefined pharmacy subjects. Your weighted average is calculated automatically based on each subject's coefficient.

Structured Calculator

For subjects with multiple exams or tests, enter grades for each. The app automatically computes subject averages and your overall grade.

Custom Calculator

Create your own grading system. Add subjects, set coefficients, choose number of exams, and optionally include TP/TD grades.

Dashboard

View overall performance across all calculators. Track your cumulative averages and subject performance with visual indicators.

Save & Export

Your data is automatically saved to your browser. Export results as PDF from the Dashboard.
